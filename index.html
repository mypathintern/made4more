Our Story

Meet Alyssa & Kylee

🩺 Occupational Therapist 📚 Special Education Teacher 💛 Best Friends & Dreamers

We've both spent our careers supporting individuals with disabilities — and over the years, we've witnessed firsthand the challenges and limitations placed on them. Not because of their abilities, but because of the systems around them.

We've also felt the limits placed on our own roles, knowing deep down that we were made for more.

That belief is the heartbeat of our mission. Rooted in community and driven by love, we're building a space where everyone is empowered to live a life full of purpose — because we are all Made 4 More.

What We Stand For

Our Mission

To empower adults with unique abilities through inclusive employment, skill development, education, and advocacy.

💼

Inclusive Employment

Creating meaningful, paid positions where every team member's unique strengths shine.

📈

Skill Development

Building real-world skills and confidence through hands-on training and mentorship.

🎓

Education & Advocacy

Changing the narrative around disability through community education and systemic advocacy.

🌟

Independence & Dignity

Fostering self-sufficiency and celebrating every person's limitless potential.

Our Vision

The Dirty Soda Shop

We're opening a dirty soda shop that's about so much more than drinks. It's a place where adults with a variety of abilities can find paid work that matches their unique strengths — from crafting sodas to greeting customers to managing inventory.
